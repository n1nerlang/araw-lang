## What is Araw?

**Araw** is a lightweight, blazing-fast programming language heavily inspired by the simplicity and speed of Lua. Just like Lua means "Moon," **Araw** brings the "Sun"—illuminating your workflow with ultra-clean syntax and lightning-fast execution. 

Designed for embedding and scripting, it takes Lua’s power and adds modern safeguards. Para sa mga devs who love minimal footprint but hate bloated frameworks, Araw gives you a fresh start without the configuration headache.

### Why Araw?

* **Lua-Infused Simplicity:** Familiar, minimalist tables and prototype-based design, pero updated for modern development.
* **Hataw sa Bilis:** Extremely lightweight engine that compiles instantly and embeds easily into C/C++ architectures.
* **Clear as Day:** Clearer syntax scoping than standard Lua—walang accidental global variables, walang dynamic scope bugs.
* **Brilliantly Portable:** Bring it anywhere. Single-binary compiler designed to keep your target systems fast and responsive.
